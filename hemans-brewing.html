Hemans Brewing

A few years ago my brother, my dad, and I thought it would be a good idea to brew our own beer. They thought it would be a good idea because they would get to drink beer they had made themselves, I thought it was a good idea because I would get to design labels for the brews. The process has been long and always evolving. This current batch of labeling is the most mature and complete. I love this project because it is truly something I have done purely for my own enjoyment, and I can periodically update or redesign it.
