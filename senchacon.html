Responsibilities:

Develop feature requirements.

Manage junior designer.

Critique iterations.

Work with development and marketing team to achieve business goals.

Description

The SenchaCon app was built to serve attendees of SenchaCon 2016 in Las Vegas. The main goal of the app was to help attendees plan which sessions they wanted to attend, and find out more about the conference. Additonally attendees could use the app to check-in to the sessions they attended and share their expereince on social media.

Goals

  • •Help users understand where and when a session takes place.
  • •Help users plan their own experience.
  • •Inform users about sessions and speakers.
  • •Gather feedback for sessions.
  • •Highlight the power of Sencha products.

Personas

  • •Our main persona was an ExtJS Developer.
  • •Typically has a few years of ExtJS experience.
  • •Wants to learn how new products and features can improve their work life.
  • •Usually sponsored by their employer.
  • •Typically works for an enterprise company.
  • •Passionate about Javascript development.

Solution

  • •Design a simple and clean interface.
  • •Allow users to create their own calendar.
  • •Rich with information, but easily digestible.
  • •Built using ExtJS, but available on Android and iOS devices.
  • •Match brand guidelines outlined by marketing.

From the built-in code editor, users can utilize the Sencha Test API and code completion to write their tests. Additionally, users can also use the event recorder to create tests with events and locators captured during their recording session.
